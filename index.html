Online banking, also known as internet banking, is a service provided by banks and financial institutions that allows customers to conduct various financial transactions over the internet. Through online banking, customers can access their bank accounts, check their balances, view transaction history, transfer funds between accounts, pay bills, and perform other banking activities without physically visiting a bank branch.

Signing up for online banking typically involves a few steps,Visit the bank Website.Typically you will need to provide your account information and create a username and password

Online banking can be safe if proper security measures are in place and users take precautions to protect their personal and financial information. Banks invest heavily in security measures to safeguard online banking transactions, including encryption technology, firewalls, and multi-factor authentication.

In online banking, customers can perform a wide range of transactions conveniently from their computer such as view account balance,tranfer fund ,open new account and so on.
